Ctrl+x : exit nano

Ctrl+w : where a file is

Ctrl+k : cuts out/deletes a certain portion of text

Ctrl+a : returns your cursor/typing point to the beginning of your text line 

Ctrl+e : directs your cursor/typing point to the end of your text line 

Esc g : allows you to search for a line or column number

Esc $ : enables soft wrapping of overlong lines
 
Esc l : enables hard wrapping of overlong lines

WHEN STARTING NANO:


-$ : nano displays the entire contents of one line across one line

-i : sets nano to tab spaces between each line

-W : nano automatically counts/treats punctuation characters as part of a word
